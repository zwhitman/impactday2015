• Impact Day

    • About

      IMPACT Day is Deloitte's firm-wide day of service in which practitioners participate in locally driven projects to support community needs. More than a day of volunteerism, IMPACT Day is a celebration of Deloitte's year-round commitment to community involvement.

      Key Dates

      - Online Registration: Friday, 5/1 to Friday, 5/8.
      - IMPACT Day 2015: Friday, June 5.

      Learn More

      Greater Washington area practitioners can check out the IMPACT Day 2015 Yammer Page for more information or to post questions.

  • How To

    • About the Map:

      The IMPACT Day map, built by Deloitte's Geospatial Analytics Practice provides a tool for practitioners to search for and learn about projects in their community.

      How to use the Map:

      Select your city: Use the City drop-down list to select the city where you will be making an IMPACT.

      Filter by event type: Use the Social Cause drop-down list to filter events by issue. You can also select the Skills-based Volunteering (SBV) option to see events that require specific skills. There is a collapsible legend in the bottom right corner that describes the icons.

      Explore sites and register: Click on an event icon to open a pop-up describing the volunteer opportunity and organization served. Click the register button to open a new window in AngelPoints to sign up. Note - you MUST be in a Deloitte office or log onto the VPN to open the AngelPoints site and register.
